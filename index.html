My Story

I relocated from the mid-west to Colorado in the 1980's. My first Colorado apartment was in Castle Rock, and from there I slowly worked my way north, living in Littleton, Denver and finally Thornton, which is where I currently reside.
The Colorado sunshine and mild winters have kept me here, though sometimes I did think it would be nice to be closer to my family. I do travel to Northern Illinois a few times a year to visit them. While there, I assist with my moms elder care and actually enjoy the break from Denver traffic.
The pandemic has changed everyday life for mmost people. I decided this was the perfect opportunity to acquire a new skillset and make a career path change. Since I find technology interesting, that seems like a logical path for me. Web pages are here to stay (for the foreseeable future), so this is the path I've chosen to begin my technology education.
